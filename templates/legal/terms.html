Terms & Conditions

Data Protection & GDPR

We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Information you provide during registration and platform use is stored securely and used only for operational, communication, and compliance purposes. You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time.

5% Commission Policy

The Bristol Regional Food Network applies a standard 5% commission on all producer sales made through the platform. This fee supports platform maintenance, customer support, and community food initiatives. Commission is deducted automatically from weekly settlements.

Weekly Payment Settlement

Payments to producers are processed on a weekly basis. Settlement reports include order totals, commission deductions, and any adjustments. Payments are issued to the bank account provided during registration. Delays may occur during bank holidays or system maintenance.

Producer Responsibilities

Producers must ensure that all product information, pricing, allergen details, and availability are accurate and up to date. Producers are responsible for fulfilling orders on time, maintaining product quality, and complying with all relevant food safety regulations.

Food Safety & Allergen Disclosure

All producers must comply with UK food safety standards, including accurate allergen disclosure as required by Natasha’s Law. Failure to provide correct allergen information may result in suspension or removal from the platform.

Environmental Sustainability Commitment

The Bristol Regional Food Network promotes sustainable and environmentally responsible practices. Producers are encouraged to minimise packaging waste, reduce carbon emissions where possible, and prioritise local sourcing.

Sandbox Payment Disclaimer

During development and testing phases, payment processing may operate in sandbox mode. Transactions made in sandbox mode are not real and no actual funds will be transferred. Users will be notified when the platform transitions to live payment processing.
